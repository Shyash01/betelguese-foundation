OUR MISSION

OUR STORY

Rooted in Execution, Driven by Purpose

Betelgeuse Foundation was established to bridge a specific gap — institutional events in India, particularly Model UN conferences and academic summits, were routinely let down by the same operational failures: poor delegate kit quality, disorganised registration, and absent accountability.

We built the systems, sourced the materials, and placed our own people on the ground. Conference by conference, we built a record — from AMIMUN '25 to RDIAS Global Summit, from USI MUN to international research conferences.

Alongside conference work, we recognised a parallel responsibility: to the communities around us. The Grassroots Fellowship, the Cyber First Responder Programme, food drives, and elderly care visits grew from the same organisational instinct — if you can coordinate an 800-delegate conference, you can coordinate a community food distribution.

We don't separate logistics excellence from social responsibility. To us, both are expressions of the same discipline.
